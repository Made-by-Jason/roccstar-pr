1st Annual Cultural Alliance Awards Honors Le'on "Rocstar" Youngblood Jr.

Los Angeles, CA – The Founder’s Community Outreach proudly announces the 1st Annual Cultural Alliance Awards, a black-tie gala celebrating cultural excellence. Scheduled for Saturday, September 20, 2025, from 2:00 PM to 10:00 PM at 3281 W 6th Street, Los Angeles, CA 90020, this event will honor Le'on "Rocstar" Youngblood Jr. with the Lifetime Achievement Award for his remarkable contributions to the music industry.

A renowned music producer, songwriter, and recording artist, Youngblood has collaborated with global icons such as Chris Brown, Post Malone, Usher, Jennifer Lopez, and Kendrick Lamar. His work on Chris Brown’s album X (2014), including the Grammy-nominated lead single "Fine China," and the Fifty Shades of Grey soundtrack (2015), featuring his song "Rude," highlight his industry impact. With nine Grammy nominations, Youngblood’s debut single "Confidence" premiered on Billboard in 2014, and he has appeared on VH1’s Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood and Basketball Wives.

Career Highlights

Youngblood began his journey with Visual and Performing Arts programs at Hollywood High School. In 2013, he partnered with Chris Brown, producing six tracks for the album X alongside Diplo and Timbaland. He founded RoccAge Entertainment and has since worked with artists like Fergie, Rita Ora, and Prince Royce. His 2023 contributions to Chris Brown’s 11:11 album, including "Summer Too Hot" and "Angel Numbers / Ten Toes," further cement his legacy.
